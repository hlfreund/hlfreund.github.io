About Me


I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Medicine at UC San Diego with Dr. Cynthia Hsu.

I have PhD in Genetics, Genomics, and Bioinformatics from UC Riverside.

My pronouns are they/them/theirs.

My postdoctoral work will focus on the gut microbiome and its connection to liver disease and alcohol use disorder.

As a genderfluid scientist, I deeply value equity and justice in STEM. For some more personal information about me and other queer & genderqueer scientists, please feel free to see my profile & others on 500 Queer Scientists.
