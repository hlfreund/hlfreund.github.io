About Me


I am a PhD Student in the Genetics, Genomics, and Bioinformatics at UCR.

I earned my MSc. in Biology from California State University Long Beach in 2019. As a member of The Bioinformatics Lab at CSULB, I used shotgun metagenomic sequencing and bioinformatic tools to examine the gut microbial community from cartilaginous fish.

I am currently a member of Dr. Emma Aronson's Soil Microbiome Lab at UCR, focusing on the characterization of aeolian (i.e. wind-driven) microbiomes.
